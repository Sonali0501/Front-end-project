    How does the Double actually work?

    Double 3 is a cloud- and app-based product. This means that you use the robot through a PC, Mac, iPad, iPhone or Android. You navigate the robot by clicking where you want the robot to go. The built-in sensors allow the robot to avoid obstacles automatically. The robot has two different speeds, can adjust its height, park, take pictures, screen share or display any site on the screen.
